About Us

Flora House understands that office desks often become spaces of pressure and busyness. That's why we're here with a solution that's not only practical but also refreshing: a collection of mini houseplants designed specifically to beautify and enliven your workspace. These tiny plants are carefully selected, considering their size, ease of care, and benefits to the work environment.

In addition to offering a wide selection of mini houseplants, Flora House also provides a variety of supporting accessories, such as cute and aesthetic mini pots, practical plant stands, and easy-to-use plant care tools. We understand that every office desk has a different style and needs, so we strive to provide a variety of options that can be tailored to your preferences. Our expert team is ready to provide consultations and advice on selecting the most suitable plants and accessories, as well as provide practical and easy-to-follow care tips.
